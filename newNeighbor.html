Our Neighborhood

Our members can truly embrace the lifestyle here! Take a walk or bike ride on the paths connecting to the various shops and restaurants! Enjoy a move at the new theater just across the street or check out a book at the new Ankeny Public Library just a short walk away.

You won't need to travel far to find all of your daily conveniences! Grocery stores, medical facilities, banking and pharmacies are just around the corner! Come and see how easy and convenient life can be by becoming a member of Vintage Cooperatives of Prairie Trail!

The District

Located in the heart of the Prairie Trail District, our 55+ residential community is just walking distance to all the amenities that Prairie Trail has to offer.

  • Cultural opportunities
  • Banks
  • Grocery stores
  • Shopping centers
  • Casual dining
  • Theaters
  • Medical clinics
  • Golf courses
  • Academic activities
  • New public library
  • Walking trails and parks
  • And many more...!
